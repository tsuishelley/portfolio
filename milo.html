Milo Treats was a DTC startup in which I was the lead designer in charge of all brand collateral. Their unfair advantage was to be the only pet supplement brand that developed custom treats tailored to your dog and their unique lifestyle.

I designed the visual identity to be refined, playful, but above all else trustworthy to fellow dog owners who want what’s best for their pups. With a playful color scheme and a memorable logo mark, the brand and its application intentionally toes the line between fun and function.

Note: This project is a refresh of the original brand after I decided to revisit and refine old assets.



I graduated from the University of Washington with a Bachelor of Design in visual communication and a minor in informatics. I’ve learned to acknowledge and embrace the design process as fluid, never fixed. Some of my past collaborators include Microsoft, Google, Amazon, Curious VC, and Pioneer Square Labs.

Most recently I’ve obtained my certificate in front-end development as a complement to my design skills. Right now I’m freelancing, working on my own passion projects, and looking for my next opportunity in visual and/or UX design.
